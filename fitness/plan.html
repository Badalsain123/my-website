WORKOUT PLAN

Day 1: Upper Body (chest and triceps)

  1. Bench Press:- (3 sets of 8-12 reps)

  3. dumbbell Flyes:- (3 sets of 12-15 reps)

  5. Abs (Hanging Leg Raises):- (3 sets of 8-12 reps)

  1. Squats (Barbell/Dumbbell):- (3 sets of 8-12 reps)

  3. Romanian Deadlifts:- (3 sets of 8-12 reps)
  4. lungs Dumbbell :- (3 sets of 12-15 reps)

  5. Dumbbell Bicep Curls:- 3 sets of 12-15 reps
